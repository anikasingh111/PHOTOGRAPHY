Getting to know me!

Hello Readers. My name is Anika Singh. I had keen interest in photography from a very young age. I started to include myself into object photography when I came to Canada in December 2022. My photography collection kept on increasing day by day therefore, I categorized my artworks into 3 categories i.e Nature photography, Architecture Photography and Sketchworks. The Sketchworks that I have included in my webpage are self-made. I would love to make you walk through my art and photography. Hope you would enjoy seeing my artworks.
